Privacy Policy

Your rights, responsibilities, and data protection in our community

Community Privacy Guidelines

The Community is maintained by members itself and not related to any organisations or firms. We believe in creating a safe, respectful environment where anime fans can connect and share their passion.

What You're Allowed To Do

As a valued member of our community, you have the following permissions and responsibilities:

  • Share Community-Related Content
    Send community-related doubts, posts, and help requests. Share your anime experiences, recommendations, and engage in meaningful discussions.
  • Non-Prohibited Topics
    Send out-of-topic discussions that aren't prohibited in our Terms of Service. This includes general entertainment, technology, and cultural discussions relevant to our community.
  • Contact Community Admins
    You're permitted to contact community active admins to talk about issues, concerns, or suggestions for improvement.
  • Content Sharing
    Share anime reviews, episode discussions, fan art, cosplay photos, and related creative content with proper credits.
  • Event Participation
    Organize and participate in community events, watch parties, and discussion sessions.
  • Cultural Exchange
    Share insights about Japanese culture, language, and traditions related to anime and manga.

Data Protection & Privacy

You're required to protect your data and information from sharing with community members. The community is a public place where other people can see your name, number, and the details shared by WhatsApp.

We recommend being cautious about the personal information you share and using WhatsApp's privacy settings to control what's visible to other members.

Important Guidelines

To maintain a healthy community environment, please follow these important guidelines:

  • Respectful Admin Communication
    Spamming on admin numbers will get you in trouble. Be gentle and more clear about your problems when contacting admins.
  • Personal Information Protection
    Avoid sharing sensitive personal information like home address, financial details, or private contact information in group chats.
  • Respect Others' Privacy
    Do not share other members' personal information without their explicit consent.
  • Content Responsibility
    You are responsible for the content you share. Ensure it doesn't violate anyone's privacy or intellectual property rights.
  • Screenshot Guidelines
    Avoid sharing screenshots of private conversations without permission from all involved parties.
  • Reporting Mechanism
    Use the proper reporting channels for privacy concerns rather than addressing them publicly in group chats.

Last Updated: 17/10/2025

Note: For complete list of prohibited activities, please refer to our Terms of Service
