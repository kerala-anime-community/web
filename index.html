About Kerala Anime Community

KAC is a vibrant community built exclusively for anime enthusiasts across Kerala. We bring together fans who share a passion for Japanese animation, culture, and storytelling.

Founded in 2020, our community has grown to include thousands of members from all districts of Kerala. We organize regular events, watch parties, discussions, and cultural exchanges.

Whether you're a seasoned otaku or just discovering the world of anime, KAC welcomes you to join our growing family of Kerala anime fans.
