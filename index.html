About Kerala Anime Community

KAC (Kerala Anime Community) is the premier destination for anime enthusiasts across Kerala. We bring together fans of anime, manga, Japanese culture, and everything related to the vibrant world of Japanese animation.

Our community is built by fans, for fans - creating a space where Malayali anime lovers can connect, share, and celebrate their passion together.
