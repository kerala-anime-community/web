Guidelines and rules for being part of Kerala Anime Community

Community Guidelines

KAC refers to Kerala Anime Community is a community built for Anime, Movies, Series, Drama fans to share and engage their activities. Our WhatsApp group (refers to groups on WhatsApp) are maintained by Community Admins.

The Community must maintain actively by following these terms:

Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited in our community. Violation may result in immediate removal from the community.

  • Advertising
    We are not created this community for sharing your earning methods, or businesses.
  • Refer Process of Applications or Websites
    Sharing referral links or promoting unrelated topics is not allowed.
  • Payment Related Services
    Any discussion or promotion of payment services is prohibited.
  • 18+ Related Contents
    This includes porn, hanime, and anime 18+ flagged images, videos, audio, documents.
  • Copyrighted Contents
    Sharing or distributing copyrighted material without permission is strictly prohibited.
  • Spamming Unnecessary Messages
    Avoid sending repetitive, irrelevant, or excessive messages that disrupt community conversations.
  • Entering Other People's Personal Messages
    We are looking for a community with privacy and security. Entering someone's DM without their permission is strictly prohibited and we will take strict Legal actions.
  • Chicking (Flirting)
    While it's a most common behavior for humans, chicking is strictly prohibited. The community isn't built for finding your future partner, and using words with such intentions will be punished.
  • Financial Begging
    We are a Community, not a financial funding group.
  • Account Exchanges
    Exchanging accounts with other community members are prohibited and we won't be responsible for any issues arising from such exchanges.
  • Buying or Selling
    Currently we haven't built a Buying and Selling platform for Any of our services, and anything related is prohibited.
  • Only Related Topics
    Please keep discussions relevant to anime, movies, series, dramas, and related topics.

Note: What you're allowed to do will be detailed in our privacy page. Please check it out for permitted activities and community benefits.
